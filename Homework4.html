My name is Kaylin ("Kay") Ridge. I am a senior at UMass Lowell pursuing a major in computer science and a minor in Italian studies. In addition to my general computer science curriculum, I have studied mobile application programming. My work Experience
During the summer of 2015, I worked as an intern at Microsoft's Charlotte campus for six weeks. I was part of a group of five students that were chosen to participate in Microsoft's first internship program for high school students outside of their main campus in Redmond, Washington. I shadowed employees and assisted with various tasks, such as repairing employee hardware and upgrading laptops to Windows 10 from Windows 8. I also assisted with Microsoft's annual "DigiGirlz" summer camp, which aims to get more young women interested in STEM. During my internship, I also practiced coding in Java and created some very basic games using Unity and C#. I also gained knowledge of culture in the workplace, such as appropriate attire and behavior, expectations during business meetings, event planning, and giving presentations.
